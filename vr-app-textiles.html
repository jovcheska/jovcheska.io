VR App for Textiles

The VR app allows potential clients to experiment with colorways, print types, trims and accessories offered by Texet (company that sells workwear, promowear, sportswear..). This empowers clients to make better informed choices about the design of their garments, while reducing the time spent in communication between clients and the company regarding the look & feel of the garment before placing and order.

How does it work?

Real life environment

Avatar

The user can view their garment on an avatar choosing between multiple models, reflective of real people within the industry. This enables them to get a more realistic idea of how the garment will fit on different body types, take a closer look at the small details and get an overall look and feel of the garment before ordering.

Garment

This gives an option to clients to see all garments that they can order and customize themselves.

Decoration

Since Texet is a company that sells textiles (workwear, promowear, sportswear...) to companies that want their branding on their garments, here they can upload their logo, decide on placement and choose the type of application ( print, embroidery...)

Customization

Clients can further develop their design with customizable zipperpullers, label, side tags, cords, buttons and necktapes.
